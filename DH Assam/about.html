A Bit About Darul Huda Assam

The First Off-Campus Extension of Darul Huda Islamic University in Northeast India

Established in March 2013 at Baisha-Barmara in Barpeta District, Darul Huda Assam Centre represents a groundbreaking initiative in Islamic education in Northeast India. As the premier off-campus extension of Darul Huda Islamic University, Kerala, we bridge South India's scholarly traditions with Assam's rich cultural heritage.

Spanning over 10 acres of dedicated educational space, our institution serves not just as an academic center but as a vibrant hub for community development, cultural preservation, and holistic growth.

Our Educational Philosophy

Rooted in tradition, reaching for the future – a balanced approach to knowledge

Community-Centered

Our Mission & Values

Guiding principles that shape our educational philosophy and community

Our Mission

Six dimensions of our mission:

Dynamic and Dutiful

To develop an Islamic educational framework to prepare religious scientists who acquire, practice and propagate Islam.

Holistic and Harmonious

To offer a holistic material education harmonious with Islamic principles.

Integrated Education

To incorporate Islamic values into educational practices.

Unified Knowledge

To unify the purpose of seeking knowledge in line with Islamic legacy.

Excellence and Innovation

To provide academic excellence of highest standards.

Productive and Self-Reliant

To offer evidence-based academic tools to build self-reliance.

Core Values

These fundamental values guide every aspect of life at Darul Huda Assam Centre.

  • Curiosity: Fostering intellectual inquiry
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethics
  • Compassion: Empathy and care for all
  • Resilience: Overcoming challenges with faith
  • Innovation: Creative thinking within Islamic framework
  • Community: Building strong bonds
  • Excellence: Highest standards in all pursuits
