Director's Message

Welcome to Darul Huda Assam Centre, where we believe in nurturing minds that bridge traditional Islamic wisdom with contemporary knowledge. Our institution stands as a beacon of holistic education, committed to developing scholars who are not only academically proficient but also morally grounded and socially responsible.

Since our establishment in 2013, we have consistently strived to create an environment where students can explore the depths of Islamic sciences while mastering modern disciplines. Our dedicated faculty, comprehensive curriculum, and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that every student receives personalized attention and guidance on their educational journey.

I invite you to join our community of learners and become part of an institution that values excellence, integrity, and service to humanity. Together, we will shape a future where knowledge serves both faith and society.

Sayyid Mueenuddin Hudawi Director, Darul Huda Assam Centre
