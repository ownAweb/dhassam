Serving Our Community

Darul Huda Assam Centre is committed to serving and uplifting our local communities through various outreach programs. Our students and staff work together to make a positive impact on society through education, healthcare, disaster relief, and environmental initiatives.

Ongoing Projects

Education & Literacy Programs

Adult Literacy Initiative

Providing basic literacy and numeracy education to adults in remote villages who missed formal schooling opportunities.

Impact: 300+ adults taught to read and write

Frequency: Weekly classes in 15 villages

After-School Tutoring

Free tutoring services for underprivileged children to help them excel in their regular school studies.

Impact: 450+ children supported annually

Subjects: Math, Science, English, Arabic

Digital Literacy Program

Teaching basic computer skills to youth and young adults to enhance their employment opportunities.

Impact: 200+ youth trained in basic computing

Equipment: Mobile computer lab with 20 laptops

Health & Wellness Initiatives

Free Medical Camps

Quarterly health camps providing free checkups, basic treatments, and medicines to remote communities.

Impact: 2,500+ patients treated annually

Partners: Local doctors and hospitals

Health Awareness Campaigns

Educational programs on hygiene, nutrition, maternal health, and preventive healthcare.

Impact: 50+ awareness sessions conducted

Topics: Sanitation, nutrition, disease prevention

Blood Donation Drives

Regular blood donation camps in partnership with local blood banks and hospitals.

Impact: 500+ units of blood collected annually

Frequency: Quarterly donation drives

Disaster Relief & Support

Flood Relief Operations

Emergency response during annual floods providing food, water, medicine, and temporary shelter to affected families.

Impact: 800+ families assisted in 2025 floods

Supplies: Food kits, water purifiers, medicines

Winter Relief Program

Distribution of warm clothes, blankets, and heating supplies to vulnerable communities during winter months.

Impact: 1,200+ winter kits distributed annually

Items: Blankets, sweaters, socks, caps

Ramadan Food Distribution

Providing essential food supplies to needy families during the holy month of Ramadan.

Impact: 600+ food packages distributed annually

Contents: Rice, flour, oil, dates, other essentials

Environmental Initiatives

Tree Plantation Drives

Annual tree plantation campaigns to combat deforestation and promote environmental conservation.

Impact: 5,000+ trees planted since 2020

Species: Indigenous and fruit-bearing trees

Clean Water Projects

Installing water purification systems and wells in villages with limited access to clean drinking water.

Impact: 8 villages provided with clean water access

Systems: 15 water purifiers installed

Clean Village Campaign

Promoting cleanliness and proper waste management in rural communities through education and infrastructure.

Impact: 12 villages adopted cleanliness practices

Activities: Waste bins, composting pits, awareness
