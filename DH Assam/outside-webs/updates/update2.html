2nd Qur'an Hizb Workshop 2025: A Journey to Flawless Recitation

PR
Publications Desk Official Correspondent

Alhamdulillah, the Islamic Studies Department successfully concluded the 2nd Qur'an Hizb Workshop 2025, a 33-day intensive program held from November 1 to December 3. This triennial event, exclusively for degree students, marked another milestone in our institution's commitment to preserving the authentic recitation of the Holy Qur'an.

Workshop Overview & Significance

The Qur'an Hizb Workshop, organized every three years, stands as a prestigious program dedicated to perfecting the recitation of the Holy Qur'an. This year's edition saw enthusiastic participation from degree students across various disciplines, all united in their pursuit of mastering Qur'anic recitation.

The primary objective of this intensive workshop is to guide students toward flawless recitation (Qira'at) and instill a profound understanding of Tajweed rules. Under the expert guidance of Ismail Hudawi Ustad, each participant completed a Khatmul Qur'an, marking a significant personal and spiritual achievement.

The heart and soul of the workshop was our esteemed teacher, Ismail Hudawi Ustad. Renowned for his expertise in Qur'anic sciences, Ustad's patient guidance and meticulous attention to detail created an ideal learning environment. His unique teaching methodology involved having students recite directly while he corrected mistakes in real-time, providing immediate, personalized feedback that accelerated learning.

"This workshop is not merely about correct pronunciation; it's about connecting with the divine words as they were revealed. Each student here is becoming a guardian of this sacred tradition, ensuring the Qur'an is recited with the perfection it deserves." – Ismail Hudawi

Curriculum & Daily Structure

The 33-day program was carefully structured to maximize learning:

Perfecting Recitation: Daily sessions began with students reciting assigned portions (Hizb). Ustad Hudawi listened attentively, correcting every vowel (harakah), elongation (madd), and stop (waqf) with precision.

Mastery of Tajweed: The science of Tajweed was taught with exceptional depth. Rules including Idgham, Ikhfa, Qalqalah, and Madd were not merely explained but practiced until correct application became second nature.

Khatmul Qur'an: Each student achieved the milestone of completing a full recitation of the Qur'an under Ustad's direct supervision, ensuring their recitation met the highest standards of authenticity.

Innovative Feature: Teachers' Workshop

In a groundbreaking development this year, the institution introduced a parallel 12-day intensive workshop for teaching faculty. Recognizing that empowering teachers amplifies knowledge transmission to future generations, this special session allowed educators to refine their own recitation and Tajweed skills under Ustad Hudawi's guidance.

Conclusion & Future Outlook

The 2nd Qur'an Hizb Workshop has, by the grace of Allah, produced a cohort of students who are now conscientious bearers of the Qur'anic recitation tradition. The addition of the teachers' workshop has created a multiplier effect that will benefit countless future students.

We extend our deepest gratitude to Ismail Hudawi Ustad for his invaluable service and inspiring mentorship. We also thank our dedicated faculty, administrative staff, and all participants for their commitment to this sacred endeavor.

We pray to Allah (SWT) to accept this effort, make the Qur'an the nourishing light of our hearts, and grant us the ability to implement its teachings in our lives. We eagerly anticipate the 3rd workshop in 2028, insha'Allah.
